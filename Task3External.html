Narsapur

Narsapur is a Town in Narsapur Mandal in West Godavari District of Andhra Pradesh State, India. It belongs to Andhra region . It is located 81 KM towards East from District head quarters Eluru. It is a Mandal head quarter.
This Place is in the border of the West Godavari District and East Godavari District.
East Godavari District Sakhinetipalle is East towards this place . It is near to bay of bengal. There is a chance of humidity in the weather.
